## Executive Summary

Asia and India represent the world's largest concentration of plastic injection moulding capacity, with manufacturing hubs in Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam, South Korea, Japan, Taiwan, Singapore, India, and the Philippines operating under increasing regulatory, ESG, and efficiency pressure. VariControl's AI-powered digital twin, drift detection, and process repeatability platform addresses a critical pain point in these markets: the growing gap between compliance documentation requirements and the manual, reactive quality systems most mid-tier moulders still operate. The 25 accounts identified in this report span medical devices, automotive, electronics, and industrial components sectors, with a geographic weighting toward ASEAN (Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam) and the mature regulated markets of South Korea, Japan, and Taiwan. Fourteen of the 25 prospects hold ISO 13485 or IATF 16949 certification, indicating they already operate in sectors where audit-ready compliance and process traceability are mandatory. Key findings include a strong pattern of recent facility expansions and growing pressure from OEM customers on quality data reporting, creating acute demand for exactly the kind of real-time process intelligence VariControl provides.

## How to Use This Report

Prioritise the HIGH confidence prospects in the medical devices sector first (Inzign, Cam Plas, THY Precision, Cephas Medical), as these companies are already under ISO 13485 regulatory pressure and are most receptive to compliance-focused messaging. The automotive cluster (Thai Usui, Jet Industries, Advance Plus, YPC, PT Banshu, CX Technology, Anil Plastics) should be approached with IATF 16949 drift detection and process repeatability hooks. For outreach sequencing, begin with Singapore and Malaysia (fastest English-language engagement), then India (English-fluent, strong SaaS adoption), then move to Vietnam and Thailand, with Japan and South Korea handled via local-language follow-up or partner-led introductions. Sectors showing strongest signal are medical devices (ISO compliance burden is acute) and automotive (OEM scorecards increasingly demand real-time quality data). Decision maker titles to target are Plant Manager and Operations Director in ASEAN markets, VP Manufacturing in India and Korea.
